# Problem Statement

## Fragmented planning problem
Travel planning is split across search, maps, chats, bookings, notes, and calendars, creating a constant need to translate information between tools.

## Travel uncertainty problem
Users face timing, route, and local-context uncertainty that generic travel content often fails to address.

## Cross-tool chaos problem
Even after making decisions, users still need to export, share, and hand plans off into other products to get things done.

## Real-time confusion problem
During a trip, users need quick clarity on what is next, what changed, and how to move without friction.

## Poor trust in generic AI answers
Travel users quickly lose trust when AI sounds polished but lacks grounded context, confidence framing, or practical next steps.
