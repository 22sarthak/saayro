# Brand Foundation

## Name meaning
Saayro should evoke motion, travel, and companionship. It is a premium travel brand first and an AI product second.

## Tagline usage
- Primary tagline: `Your trip's smarter half`
- Best used in onboarding, launch, and high-level marketing
- Avoid repeating it on every screen

## Logo intent
- clean wordmark first
- optional route or horizon motif
- no robot mascots or dark futuristic language

## Brand personality
- calm
- premium
- intelligent
- supportive
- editorial
- trustworthy

## Emotional tone
Saayro should feel reassuring in moments of uncertainty and quietly capable in moments of action.

## Language do and don't
- Do speak clearly, warmly, and with practical guidance
- Do make travel decisions easier to scan
- Don't use robotic phrasing or hype-heavy AI language
- Don't imply certainty when the product is estimating
