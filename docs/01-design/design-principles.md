# Design Principles

## Calm over clutter
Use whitespace, hierarchy, and sequencing to reduce travel stress.

## Intelligence over gimmick
Show intelligence through relevance, structure, and clarity rather than flashy assistant chrome.

## Travel-first, not AI-first chrome
Primary surfaces should foreground trip state, routes, stops, and actions.

## Premium but approachable
The product should feel refined without becoming cold or overly minimal.

## Assistive, never controlling
Buddy should guide, suggest, and clarify while leaving decisions visible and editable.

## Portable outputs over lock-in
The product must cooperate with maps, notes, messaging, and exports.
