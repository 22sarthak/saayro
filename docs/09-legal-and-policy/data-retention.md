# Data Retention

## Chat history
Retain only as long as needed to support trip context and user-visible history, subject to future policy finalization.

## Exports
Keep export metadata and artifacts only for a defined convenience window or until user deletion, depending on final product policy.

## Connected item cache
Cache connected travel items only as long as needed for the connected feature experience and sync accuracy.

## Uploaded media
Retain uploaded media only when required for the user-visible feature or support workflow.

## Deleted-account behavior
Account deletion should remove or anonymize retained user data according to the final legal policy and operational feasibility.
