# Disclaimer Strategy

## Travel timing disclaimer
Travel times can change based on traffic, delays, local conditions, and provider updates.

## Route disclaimer
Route guidance and map handoffs depend on third-party providers and should be confirmed when timing is critical.

## Safety disclaimer
Local conditions can vary. Saayro should avoid presenting safety-sensitive guidance as guaranteed.

## Translation confidence disclaimer
Translated or interpreted content should include confidence-aware wording when ambiguity remains.
