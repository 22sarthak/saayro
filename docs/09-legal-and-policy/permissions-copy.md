# Permissions Copy

## Camera
`Use your camera to capture travel details, signs, or booking info when you want Buddy to help interpret them.`

## Mic
`Use your microphone to speak to Buddy hands-free when typing is inconvenient.`

## Notifications
`Get timely updates about trip actions, reminders, and important changes when you choose to enable them.`

## Inbox access
`Connect your inbox so Saayro can help surface travel-related confirmations and organize them into your trip. You can disconnect this anytime.`

## Calendar access
`Connect your calendar to review trip events in context and keep travel plans easier to manage. You stay in control and can revoke access anytime.`
