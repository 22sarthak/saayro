# Beta Plan

## Tester profile
- frequent travelers in India
- users who coordinate trips for others
- users willing to give structured product feedback

## Beta goals
- validate trust and clarity
- validate usefulness of trip organization flows
- find friction in auth, exports, and handoff flows

## Feedback collection format
- short in-product prompts later
- structured bug intake
- periodic qualitative interviews

## Bug severity definitions
- critical: blocks a core trip journey
- major: serious degradation with limited workaround
- medium: noticeable issue with workaround
- minor: cosmetic or low-impact issue
