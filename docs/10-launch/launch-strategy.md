# Launch Strategy

## Launch audience
- early adopters who travel frequently in India
- premium convenience-seeking planners
- users who coordinate trips for others

## City strategy
- begin with demand-rich travel use cases and cities where route, dining, and coordination pain is strong
- expand once operational confidence improves

## Positioning message
Saayro is your trip's smarter half: a calm, premium AI travel companion for planning, organizing, and moving with confidence.

## Acquisition channels
- landing page
- referrals and founder-led outreach
- travel creator or community pilots
- closed beta invites
